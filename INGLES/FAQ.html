Frequently Asked Questions

How do I order spare parts?
Contact your sales representative with the VIN and part number. Orders are processed within 24 hours.
What is the delivery time?
Standard delivery: 3-5 business days (US), 2-4 days (UK). Express options available.
How do I report a defect?
Use the complaint form on our website. Include photos and the VIN. Response within 24-48 hours.
Where are technical manuals?
Technical documentation is available on our support portal. Contact support for access.
What is the warranty period?
Standard parts: 12 months. Powertrain: 24 months. Proof of purchase required.
Can I cancel an order?
Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours if not in production. Contact your sales rep immediately.
How do I track my shipment?
Tracking information is sent by email. You can also check status on our customer portal.
What payment methods do you accept?
Bank transfer, corporate credit cards, and purchase orders (approved accounts only).
